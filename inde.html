FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about medical tourism

What is medical tourism and how does it work?

Medical tourism is traveling abroad for medical treatment. We handle everything from consultation to recovery, including travel arrangements, hospital bookings, and follow-up care. You save 40-70% on costs while receiving world-class treatment.

How much money can I save with Gods Life Health Care?

Patients typically save 40-70% on medical procedures compared to costs in Western countries. This includes treatment, travel, accommodation, and our full support services. Use our cost calculator above for specific savings on your treatment.

Are the doctors and hospitals internationally accredited?

Yes, all our partner hospitals are JCI, NABH, or ISO certified. Our doctors are internationally trained with years of experience and hold certifications from recognized medical boards worldwide.

What support do you provide during my medical journey?

We provide complete end-to-end support: free consultation, medical record review, hospital/doctor selection, visa assistance, flight booking, airport pickup, accommodation, interpreter services, 24/7 support in your language, and follow-up care after you return home.

How do I get started with Gods Life Health Care?

Getting started is easy! Click "Book Free Consultation" to connect with our medical experts. Share your medical records, and we'll create a personalized treatment plan with cost estimates from multiple hospitals. There's no obligation, and consultations are completely free.

Is my medical information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We are ISO 27001 certified and NABH accredited, ensuring the highest standards of data security and patient privacy. Your medical information is encrypted and never shared without your explicit consent.
